## The Access Problem (And How to Solve It)

Here's the thing that trips people up: if you're in the US, you can only access Binance USA, which is a stripped-down version of the full Binance exchange. You can't just buy BNB directly the way you might buy ETH on Coinbase.

But there's a workaround that's pretty straightforward:

1. Buy ETH on whatever exchange you're already using
2. Use the Binance Bridge to port that ETH over to the Binance Smart Chain
3. Swap your ETH for BNB on PancakeSwap

Yes, it's an extra step. Yes, there are gas fees involved. My advice? Just make a little extra ETH budget room to cover the bridging costs and give it a go. The friction is worth pushing through, because once you're on BSC, an entire universe of tokens opens up.
